Support for the seasons that feel heavy.

When life changes, our homes often feel it first. Belongings pile up. Decision-making feels heavy. Emotional attachments can make it hard to know what stays and what goes. Good Grief Home Organization Co. exists to offer calm, hands-on support with no judgment, no rushing, and no pressure.

Serving: Lake Cities and surrounding areas

Approach: Grief-informed, trauma-aware, and client-led

Support Style: Work side-by-side or independently with your guidance

Focus: Decluttering, organizing, sentimental sorting, and life transitions

Travel: Additional travel available for an added fee

frequently asked questions

What is Good Grief Co.?

  • Hands-on decluttering and home support
  • Grief-informed, trauma-aware, and client-led
  • Practical support offered with compassion

What is it not?

  • Not therapy or mental health treatment
  • Not crisis intervention
  • Not a cleaning service
  • Not a moving company

How do sessions work?

  • Start with the intake form
  • Then schedule a brief phone conversation
  • Choose a date that feels comfortable
  • Sessions are scheduled in 4-hour blocks
  • Everything moves at the client’s pace

What are your policies?

  • Payment is due at the end of each session
  • 24-hour notice is requested for rescheduling
  • All client decisions are final and respected
  • No items are removed without explicit consent
  • Privacy is always respected
